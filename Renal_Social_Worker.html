Renal Social Worker

Primary responsibility is the psychosocial assessment, consideration and implementation of interventions appropriate for ESRD patient and their families. To assist in understanding Medicare/Medicaid responsibilities. Additional responsibilities include continuity of care management to include assessments, plan implementations, monitor and evaluated operations and services to meet patient’s health needs. State license in Clinical Social Work, Master’s Degree in Social Work (MSW), 2 years experience as SW in health care setting.
