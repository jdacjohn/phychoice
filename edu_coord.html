Education Coordinator

Responsible for the planning, implementation and evaluation of all hemodialysis training of new employees; participates in the orientation of new employees with previous dialysis experience, arranges and assures current CPR certification for all clinical and technical staff; assists hemodialysis technicians with educational needs in preparation for certification exam if applicable; assists in the development of patient educational materials; fosters staff growth via in-services and other educational means, and assists the Facility Administrator with staff or facility educational needs. Valid RN License, 3 years RN experience with at least 2 years in care of renal patient and 6 months in dialysis care and education. Current CPR and EKG Certification.
